About us



This website allows individuals to share a ride together in a single vehicle for a specific route or destination and enables users to create a profile and provide information about their starting point, destination, and time of travel. . The website matches individuals who are traveling in the same direction and provides them the option to share the ride together. The website aims to reduce traffic congestion, reduce the number of vehicles on the road, and promote a more eco-friendly way of traveling. Vehicle pooling helps users save money on fuel and maintenance expenses and promotes socialization with other riders. The website may offer various features, such as a search bar to find rides that match your travel route and destination and options to book rides. The website may also include a rating system that allows users to rate their experience with other riders and provide feedback. Overall, a website that operates on vehicle pooling method can be an efficient and cost-effective way to travel, and it promotes a more sustainable and eco-friendly way of commuting.
